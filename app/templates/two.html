Two of These People Are Lying

A WIP app to facilitate playing a version of Technical Difficulties: Two of These People Are Lying.

All but one player enters the title of a Wikipedia article, and the remaining player picks one at random.

The players are then to describe what the article is about and the chooser is to figure out who actually knows what the article is.

It will have a login feature to allow for remote players.

This project doesn't have a publicly accessable page at the moment.
