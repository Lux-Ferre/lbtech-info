Me

I am Chris, a full-stack developer based in Aberdeen, Scotland.

I almost exclusively go by the username Lux-Ferre on all platforms.

Mostly, I work in the digital space, building and consulting on software.

I specialise in building Python backends, with largely utilitarian front-ends. Though usually a web deployment set of technologies, I have found this to work great for local applications as well.

I also have some experience with hardware projects.

I have designed and made things with small-scale electronics (eg a 4-bit adder) sometimes including the use of microcontrollers.

Recently, I have used the Solidworks CAD software to create models and 3D printed them.

Outside of all the making things, I am a gamer, and HEMA martial artist.

This Site

This site is mostly a portfolio and hub for my work; a place to create and share what I am currently working, and have previously worked, on.

The front-end makes use of Bootstrap for responsiveness; and is served from a Flask back-end.

GitHub is used to facilitate the CI pipeline for the site, and you can view the repo here.
